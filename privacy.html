Privacy Policy

Overview

Backwater Journal is designed to help users record fishing activity and connect with other anglers. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we may collect, how we may use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information, journal entries, catch details, photos, location-related information you choose to provide, device information, and communications you send to us.

How We Use Information

We may use information to provide and improve the app, maintain accounts, personalize features, support community functions, respond to requests, and protect the safety and integrity of the service.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or when you choose to make content visible to other users.

Your Choices

You may update account details, manage what you share, or contact us about privacy requests. Some app features may require certain information to function.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to hello@backwaterjournal.com.
